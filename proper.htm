Property rights are a legal mechanism used to secure the non-human inputs and outputs of production.

Future input property commitments are Sources.
Finished output property commitments are goods.

"Properly Rights" (sometimes "Property Left") is a constrained form of property rights created when owners choose to apply the IOTA to their property.

Most property can usually be purchased with money.
