RoboJoe:  One touch, self cleaning coffee maker.

Hopper holds some large amount of coffee beans.
Single serving delivered to grider which empties into permanent screen.
Instant hot water or milk fills the screen and empties into the drinking cup below.
Piston presses last drops from grounds then dumps into compost collector.
Screen is backflushed with cold water to remove remaning grounds.
Brushes, a bit of soap and hot water to clean, rinse and air-dry.

Optional roasting module attaches between grinder and hopper which then stores green beans.

Other names: CoffeeBot
