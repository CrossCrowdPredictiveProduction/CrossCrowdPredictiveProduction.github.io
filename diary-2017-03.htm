2017-03-29: Business Model idea to pay for Free Software development:
. Find a Free Software app or game you think has potential to be popular.
. Choose a new name and artwork to avoid Trademark issues.
. Download source code and all artwork.
. Change name and design new artwork or replace with placeholders.
. Change software to include a button to allow users to pre-purchase of bugfixes in a sort of micro-crowdfunding manner.

2017-03-19: We can use property to preallocate product.  In this way we regain control and eliminate profit.
